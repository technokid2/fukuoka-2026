💡 Essential Tips

📋 IDP for Car Rental

Apply at Singapore AA before departure. Takes ~2 weeks. Required alongside Singapore licence for Japan car rental.

🔥 Book Robata 1 Month Ahead

Only 22 seats. Book via Instagram @robata.itookashi0411. Breakfast 8am–2pm weekdays. Dinner 5pm–11pm.

🚣 Takachiho Boat — Set Alarm

Opens exactly 9am JST, 14 days before visit. Weekends sell out in minutes. Official Takachiho Tourism website only.

♨ Ryokan Rooms Sell Out Fast

Private onsen rooms in Kurokawa and Yufuin sell out months ahead for Aug/Sep. Book as soon as flights confirmed.

🌋 Check Aso Crater Alert

Check Japan Meteorological Agency (JMA) 1–2 days before Day 5. Crater closes at Level 2+.

💴 Carry Cash

Yatai, Tempura Hirao, Robata are cash-only. Withdraw ¥80,000–100,000 at 7-Eleven airport ATMs.

🛣 ETC Card for Tolls

Request ETC card at rental counter (~¥550/day). Handles all expressway tolls automatically.

🚂 JR Kyushu Northern Pass

3-Day Pass (¥4,000) covers Nagasaki Shinkansen, Beppu Sonic, and Yufuin no Mori. Buy on Klook before departure.

🌡 August Heat

30–33°C with humidity. Pack UV shirts, portable fan, SPF 50+. Do outdoor activities before noon.

🔄 Circular Route

Starts and ends near Fukuoka Airport. Day 1 arrive → loop south → return to city → Day 12 depart same airport.
